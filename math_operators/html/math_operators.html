Couple Basics about JS - math operators

In JS is 6 main math operators

  1. "+"
  2. "-"
  3. "*"
  4. "/"
  5. "="
  6. "**"

And couple "shorthand methods".

  1. += a - is equal to number plus a
  2. -= a - is equal to number minus a
  3. *= a - is equal to number times a
  4. /= a - is equal to number divided a
  5. number++ - is equal to number + 1
  6. ++number - is equal to 1 + number
  7. number-- - is equal to number - 1
  8. --number - is equal to 1 - number
