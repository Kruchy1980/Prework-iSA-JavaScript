Operators of different type are very often used in JS code, dependly of needs - what and when the code should take specified actions by us.

Comparison operators - are used to set equality to two values.

  1. > - more than
  2. < - less than
  3. >= - more or equal to
  4. >= - less thn equal to
  5. == - eqal to (only value)
  6. === - equal to (type and value)
  7. != - different than (value only)
  8. !== - different than (type and value)
